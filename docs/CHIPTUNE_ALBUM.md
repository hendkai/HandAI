# HandAI Chiptune Select

`HandAI Chiptune Select` is a curated offline soundtrack for the RG35XXSP
interface. It replaces the early procedural demos with complete, professionally
arranged CC0 pieces by **Zane Little Music**.

| # | Track | HandAI screen | Upstream asset |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Flowerbed Fields | Main menu | `flowerbed_fields.ogg` |
| 2 | Apple Cider | Claude | `apple_cider.ogg` |
| 3 | Pixel Sprinter | Codex | `pixel_sprinter_loop.ogg` |
| 4 | The Cool Factor | Hermes | `the_cool_factor_loop.wav` |
| 5 | Heroic Loop | OpenCode | `heroic_loop.wav` |
| 6 | Void Estate | OpenClaw | `void_estate.ogg` |

HandAI changes tracks automatically when a provider home opens and returns to
`Flowerbed Fields` in the main menu. **Settings -> Music / Chiptune Album**
controls playback, digital music volume and manual track preview.

The bundled copies are normalized to mono 22.05 kHz, 16-bit PCM WAV for
dependency-free playback through PipeWire, ALSA or FFplay. The original
compositions, source pages and exact download URLs are recorded in
`handai/assets/music/SOURCE.md`.

All six compositions are released under **CC0 1.0 Universal**. Attribution is
not legally required, but HandAI credits the composer prominently because it is
the right thing to do. HandAI's source code remains under its repository
license; the audio assets retain their documented CC0 status.
